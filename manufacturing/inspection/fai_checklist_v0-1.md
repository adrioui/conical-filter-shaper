> Return signed copy to vendor with any re-work instructions.

## 1. Material Verification

- [ ] Material cert (mill cert or CoC) received and matches BOM spec
- [ ] Material grade confirmed on cert (e.g., 316L not 316; POM-C not POM-H)

---

## 3. Surface Finish

- [ ] Filter contact faces verified Ra ≤ 0.8 µm (Shell halves) or 0.4 µm (Tip Insert)
- [ ] Cam track floor Ra ≤ 1.6 µm
- [ ] Ejection rod OD Ra ≤ 0.4 µm

---

## 4. Anodize (Aluminium parts only)

- [ ] Anodize type confirmed: Type II (not Type III unless specified)
- [ ] Tapped holes: threads clear, no anodize bridging
- [ ] Detent dimples: depth confirmed post-anodize (1.4 ± 0.1 mm)
- [ ] Colour uniform, no pitting, no burning

---

## 5. Functional Checks

- [ ] Cam ring rotates smoothly in housing bore (no binding)
- [ ] Detent clicks at 3 positions with clear tactile feel
- [ ] Detent hold: ring does not rotate under 5 N tangential load at rim
- [ ] Ejection rod slides freely in bore; snaps back under spring
- [ ] Overlap fin inserts/slides in slot; pull-out force 6–14 N
- [ ] Shell halves hinge freely on tip insert block; no binding at any angle
- [ ] All fasteners torqued; no proud fastener heads on filter contact surfaces

---

## 6. Sharp Edge / Burr Check

- [ ] All filter-contact surfaces free of burrs, scratches, raised tool marks
- [ ] Tip dimple: R ≥ 0.5 mm on all edges (no sharp transition)
- [ ] Shell seam edge: 0.2 mm chamfer present
- [ ] No sharp internal corners in cam track (45° chamfer at entry)

---

## 7. Disposition

- [ ] **ACCEPT** — all checks pass; proceed with batch
- [ ] **ACCEPT WITH REWORK** — see notes below; re-inspect before batch
- [ ] **REJECT** — dimensional or material nonconformance; do not proceed
